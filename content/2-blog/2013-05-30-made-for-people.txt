Yellow is for people who make websites. Create small web pages, blogs and wikis. The focus is on people and that it's useful for you. No database, no admin panel, no upload manager, no complex framework. Most people don't need all of this. Let's concentrate on the essential aspects, on people and tools that work together. If you are interested in simple things, find out more. [--more--]
