Coffee is a beverage made from the roasted beans of the coffee plant. It's also the greatest addiction of the modern world. It's pure awesome.

1. Start with fresh coffee. Coffee beans start losing quality immediately after roasting and grinding. The best coffee is made from beans ground right after roasting. Buy your favorite coffee. Grind the beans if possible.
2. Brew a cup of coffee. Coffe is prepared with different methods and additional flavorings such as milk and sugar. There are Espresso, Filter coffee, French press, Moka pot, Turkish coffee and many more. Find out what's your favorite.
3. Enjoy.
